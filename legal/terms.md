The Ecosystem provides two primary categories of participation: (a) Signal Discovery, and (b) Depositing. Each is described below.

Signal Discovery

Participants may use the Company’s open-source software library (the “Library”) to discover predictive trading signals (“Signals”) through automated search. Signal discovery involves the following activities:

Registration. To participate, you must register a compatible self-custodial wallet with the Ecosystem. Registration is free and does not require any monetary contribution.

Data Access. Upon registration, participants may download pre-computed market data (“Features”) provided by the Company. The Company retains full discretion over which Features are made available and may modify, add, or remove Features at any time without notice.

Signal Search. Participants use the Library to construct and evaluate mathematical expressions (“Expressions”) that combine Features and transformation functions. The Library runs entirely on the participant’s own hardware. The Company does not provide computational resources for Signal search.

Signal Submission. Expressions that meet locally-evaluated quality thresholds may be submitted to the Company’s servers for independent validation. The Company applies its own proprietary validation criteria to determine whether a submitted Signal is accepted. The Company’s validation criteria, including any thresholds, evaluation periods, and acceptance conditions, are determined at the Company’s sole discretion and are not disclosed to participants. The Company reserves the right to reject any submission for any reason.

Strategy Search. Participants who have demonstrated sustained contribution to the Ecosystem, as determined by criteria set at the Company’s sole discretion, may become eligible to construct strategies (“Strategies”) that combine multiple accepted Signals. Strategies are subject to the same submission and validation process as Signals.

Eras. The Ecosystem operates in versioned periods (“Eras”). Each Era defines the available Features, transformation functions, validation parameters, and evaluation periods. The Company may transition to a new Era at any time, which may change any or all of these parameters. Participants are responsible for adapting to Era changes.
